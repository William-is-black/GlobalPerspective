Key Differences & Lessons

  • Government Regulation: Japan enforces strict pollution laws, while India struggles with enforcement.
  • Technology & Infrastructure: Japan has advanced wastewater treatment, whereas India lacks proper infrastructure.
  • Public Participation: Japan actively involves communities, while India needs more awareness campaigns.
